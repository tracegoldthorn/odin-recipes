Butter Chicken

Marinade

  • 3-4 Chicken Breasts cut into bite size pieces
  • 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 12g of Ginger
  • 1 Spoonful of kosher salt, black pepper, garam masala, smoked paprika and turmeric
  • A few heaping spoonfuls of greek yogurt. Enough to get a thick coat on the chicken.

Mix everything in a bowl until homogenous, and then place in the refridgerator until you're ready to cook it.

Sauce

Ingredients

  • Half an onion
  • Spoonful of cashew butter (peanut butter will work)
  • Same spices as before
  • Two heaping spoonfuls of greek yogurt
  • 400g can of diced tomatoes

Preperation

Saute the onion, once it has color you can add the rest of the ingredients to the pan. Once it is homogenous and warm, add it to the blender and blend until smooth.

Preparing the Dish

  1. Preheat a pan and add a light coat of oil to the bottom
  2. Add the marinaded chicken, and sear on all sides
  3. Add the sauce, then cover the dish and let sit until the chicken is cooked all the way through
  4. Serve over rice with cilantro if desired
