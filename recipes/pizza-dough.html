Homemade Pizza Dough

This is a very simple, quick pizza dough recipe made to be cooked in a cast iron pizza. It has very few ingredients and a very short preparation/cook time.

ingredients

  • 50g Flour
  • 390g Flour
  • 1 packet active dry rapid-rise yeast
  • 9g salt
  • 14g honey
  • 295ml lukewarm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, mix Â½ cup flour with yeast and salt. Dissolve honey in lukewarm water and add to mixture. Add olive oil.
  2. In the bowl of a mixer, mix for 3 minutes. Mix in remaining flour (dough should be only slightly sticky).
  3. After adding the flour, continue to mix for 5 minutes.
  4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let rise for 10 minutes in a warm place.
  5. Punch dough down and divide in half. Allow to rise for 10 additional minutes. Punch down and spread one of the halves by hand or roller onto your Cast Iron Baking Pan.
  6. Add toppings of your choice and place in a preheated 450 degrees Fahrenheit oven for 8-10 minutes, or until crust is golden brown. Cool 2-3 minutes before cutting or serving.
